Vienna

Rating: 8.5/10

Vienna is the capital of Austria filled with the atmosphere and spirit of the arts. The city has architecture from the past but also many modern buildings. We enjoyed eating hotdog and late night walks around the city after watching an extremely long opera show that we did not understand.

Top Attractions

  • Schönbrunn Palace
  • St. Stephen's Cathedral
  • Belvedere Palace

Top Restaurants

  • Plachutta (Viennese)
  • Steirereck (Fine Dining)
  • Café Central (Cafe)

Song of the City

Hallstatt

Rating: 10/10

Hallstatt is a small town in Austria that is famous for its production of salt. Traveling to Hallstatt from Vienna felt magical as we had many interesting incidents. The salt mine, glaciers, and views are amazing. Hallstatt is ones of our favorite places that we visited this trip.

Top Attractions

  • Salzwelten
  • Dachstein Glacier
  • Hallstatt Skywalk

Top Restaurants

  • Heritage Hotel Restaurant (Austrian)
  • See Hotel Restaurant (Austrian)
  • Rudolfsturm (Austrian)

Song of the City

Salzburg

Rating: 9/10

Salzburg is known as the setting for the famous film "The Sound of Music" and the birthplace of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. The small city is relaxing and peaceful and fun to walk around. We thought that Salzburg was the perfect place to end our trip before we fly to Portugal for our project.

Top Attractions

  • Marko-Feingold-Steg
  • Fortress Hohensalzbrug
  • Salzburg Marionette Theatre

Top Restaurants

  • Esszimmer (Austrian)
  • Random Hotdog Stand
  • Cafe Konditorei Furst (Cafe)
