Summer of 2022

In May of 22, I suddenly had an urge to travel with a friend to Europe. I have been to Europe with my mother before the summer of my freshman year in high school. We went to Spain, Germany, England, and switzerland. However, due to COVID, I have not been anywhere other than China and America in the past few years. Since COVID have been getting better, I wanted to revisit Europe. With less than a week to prepare for our trip, I woke up every day with the mission to create the perfect itinerary.
