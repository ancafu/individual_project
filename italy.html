Rome

Rating: 10/10

Rome is the capital of Italy filled with ancient history, art, architecture, and beauty. Rome was the first city that my friend and I visited. The number of attractions in Rome felt endless, but they were all worthy. Rome is definitely one of the best cities that we visited.

Top Attractions

  • Vatican City
  • Colosseum
  • St. Peter's Basilica

Top Restaurants

  • L'Isola della Pizza (Pizza & Steaks)
  • Saltimbocca Ristorante (Italian)
  • Osteria Barberini (Pasta)

Song of the City

Florence

Rating: 8.5/10

Florence is the second city in Italy that my friend and I visited. The city is known for its art, Renaissance architecture and cultural heritage. For my friend and me, the city was relaxing and fun to walk around. There were many memorable restaurants and food in Florence.

Top Attractions

  • Uffizi Gallery
  • Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore
  • Pitti Palace

Top Restaurants

  • Sergio Pollini Lampredotto (Sandwhich)
  • All'Antico Vinaio (Paninis)
  • Trattoria Dall'Oste (Steak House)

Song of the City

Venice

Rating: 8.5/10

Venice is the third city that my friend and I visited. I was excited to see the city as it is built on water. Even though the city was beatiful, it was not as nice as I thought. However, my friend and I still had a lot of fond memories of wandering between different islands and alleys.

Top Attractions

  • Saint Mark's Basilica
  • Bridge of Sighs
  • La Biennale di Venezia

Top Restaurants

  • Suso Ice Cream (Ice Cream)
  • Lineadombra (Italian)
  • Trattoria Al Gazzettino (Italian)
